Accent perception test

This experiment tests how small differences in the way we speak can communicate something about who we are. Please read the following instructions carefully:

A group of actors are auditioning for a TV series set in York. You will hear recordings of the actors reading single words, and see a set of characters represented by photographs.

Each time you hear a word, try to guess which character the actor is trying to portray. Your answers will be timed, so try to respond as fast as possible.

After the experiment has finished, you will be asked some questions about the people who you saw in the pictures.
